Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-16

We built BlueDot for people who care deeply about privacy and control. We collect the minimum data needed to run the product, support customers, and keep things secure. We do not sell or rent your data, and we do not use it to train AI models.

1. Data you provide

  • Account or license information (such as your email, license key, or organization name).
  • Support communications and any files you choose to share with us.
  • Payment details handled by payment processors (we do not store full payment card numbers).

2. Data we collect automatically

  • Basic device and version metadata needed to keep the app running (OS version, app version, crash logs if enabled).
  • Limited usage signals to improve reliability and prevent abuse (feature usage counts, performance metrics, and error codes).
  • Activity log metadata designed for audit and troubleshooting (timestamps and actions). These logs are intended to avoid secrets or full command output.

3. Data we do not collect

  • Your cloud credentials, private keys, or secret tokens.
  • The contents of your cloud resources, databases, or files unless you explicitly send them to support.

4. How we use data

  • Operate and secure BlueDot, including license validation and fraud prevention.
  • Provide support and respond to customer requests.
  • Improve stability and performance.

5. Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell your data. We share data only with trusted service providers who help operate the product (such as hosting, email, and payments) and only as required by law or to protect customer safety.

6. Data retention and deletion

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service, meet legal obligations, or resolve disputes. You can request deletion of your account data by contacting support.

7. Security

We use encryption in transit, access controls, and least-privilege policies to protect data. No system is perfect, but we treat your data as if it were our own.

8. Your rights

You can request access, correction, export, or deletion of your personal data. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

9. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email the team at support@browserbox.io.
